How to Write Dialogue

Tips for Writing Dialogue:

  • Character Switching: Add a blank line (press Enter twice) in a character's dialogue to indicate switching to the other character.
  • Delivery Styles: Add delivery instructions in parentheses at the end of lines:
    • Example: "Hello there!" (excited)
    • Example: "What do you want?" [angry]
    • Example: "Come closer..." "whispered"
  • Available Styles: excited, whispered, angry, calm, emphatic, quiet, gentle, shouting
